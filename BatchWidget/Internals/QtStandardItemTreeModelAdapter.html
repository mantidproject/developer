QtStandardItemTreeModelAdapter¶

The QtStandardItemTreeModelAdapter is a wrapper around QStandardItemModel which helps to enforce the strong typing on QModelIndexs. It eliminates some of the boilerplate required when working with the model in the Job Tree View, aiming to prevent JobTreeView and higher level classes from working directly with QStandardItems.

Its header also contains definitions for modelItemFromIndex whose usage in its raw form is discouraged outside of the implementation of QtStandardItemTreeModelAdapter but currently necessary in CellDelegate.

Usage¶

The QtStandardItemTreeModelAdapter is used when performing model manipulations in the JobTreeView.
